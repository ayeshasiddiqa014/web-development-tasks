Admissions

COMSATS University Islamabad (CUI), a leading Degree Awarding Institution of higher education in Pakistan, is among the Centers of Excellence of Commission on Science and Technology for Sustainable Development in the South (COMSATS).

Admission Programs

CUI Islamabad campus offers admissions twice a year; in Fall and Spring. Fall admissions tentatively commence in June and spring admissions tentatively commence in October/ November of each Year. We offer a wide range of academic programs to diverse interests and career goals.
