About Us

The first official Alumni body of COMSATS Islamabad is dedicated to keeping you connected with your campus and fellow CUI-Islamabad alumni, regardless of where you are in the world. The COMSATS-Islamabad Alumni, comprising graduates from all academic programs at CUI-Islamabad, forms an integral part of the University. This ever-expanding body represents a rich resource base for the campus with its vast reservoir of diverse talents and expertise spanning areas like Computer Sciences, Management, Engineering, and Mathematics. Through this alumni body, the campus ensures a strong communication link is established amongst the alumni and between the alumni and the campus. To achieve this, it has established an office where administrative facilities are provided to its alumni for the completion of their membership. This office is responsible for building a cohesive network of CUI-Islamabad Alumni. It ensures unhindered communication throughout the year that includes timely announcement of all CUI campuses events. The office aims to organize various alumni-based activities that will help connect the alumni around the globe. These measures keep our alumni informed about the whereabouts and achievements of fellow alumni, as well as important developments at the campus. An electronic version of the campus Magazine will also be available on the website.
